About Me

I am Mohammed Arif, a passionate and dedicated Information Technology student with a strong interest in Web Development, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning. I enjoy creating modern, user-friendly websites and developing innovative solutions that solve real-world problems.

Throughout my academic journey, I have worked on several innovative projects that helped me strengthen my practical development skills. One of my major projects is the Medicare Portal, a full-stack healthcare platform developed using React, Node.js, and Firebase for secure medical record management. In this project, I implemented role-based access for doctors, patients, and pharmacists, along with AI-based anomaly detection for prescription validation.

I also developed a Notes Application using React, Node.js, and Firebase, where users can create, update, delete, and manage notes in real time with secure authentication. The application features a responsive user interface and seamless data synchronization for a smooth user experience. These experiences have helped me build confidence, teamwork, and practical industry knowledge.

My goal is to grow as a skilled software professional, work in a reputed company, and achieve a strong position in the technology field. I believe in continuous learning, hard work, and creating meaningful digital experiences through technology. I strongly believe that hard work, consistency, and continuous learning are the keys to success. I am always ready to take new challenges, learn from experiences, and grow both personally and professionally in the field of technology.
